The Plan of Salvation: Our Eternal Journey

Heavenly Father has a loving plan for each of us. This site offers a simple, Christ-centered overview of the Plan of Salvation with scriptures, reflections, and visual summaries to help you understand your eternal journey.

Explore the Plan Study & Reflect

Why the Plan of Salvation Matters

The Plan of Salvation answers questions like “Where did I come from?”, “Why am I here?”, and “Where am I going after this life?”. As you study, consider how Jesus Christ stands at the center of this plan and how His Atonement brings meaning and hope to every part of our journey.

Timeline of Our Eternal Journey

Swipe horizontally on a phone or drag with your mouse to see each step.

  1. Premortal Life

    We lived as spirit children of Heavenly Parents and accepted the plan of happiness.

  2. Earth Life

    We receive a body, make covenants, and learn to follow Jesus Christ by faith.

  3. Spirit World

    After death, our spirits go to the spirit world where the gospel is preached to all.

  4. Resurrection & Judgment

    Through Christ we are resurrected and lovingly judged according to our works and desires.

  5. Kingdoms of Glory

    We inherit a degree of glory—celestial, terrestrial, or telestial—according to our choices.
