Study & Reflection

Use these scripture suggestions and reflection questions as a personal study guide. You can also review the reflection you submitted on the home page.

Scripture Focus

  • Premortal Life: Jeremiah 1:5; Abraham 3:22–23
  • Purpose of Mortality: 2 Nephi 2; Alma 34
  • The Atonement: Alma 7:11–13; 3 Nephi 11
  • Resurrection: Alma 40–42
  • Kingdoms of Glory: 1 Corinthians 15; Doctrine and Covenants 76

Tip: Choose one passage, read it slowly, and write a sentence about what it teaches you about Christ.

Reflection Prompts

  1. How does knowing you lived with Heavenly Father before this life change the way you see yourself?
  2. In what ways has Jesus Christ helped you during difficult moments of mortality?
  3. How does the hope of resurrection influence the way you face trials and loss?
  4. What kingdom of glory do you desire to inherit, and what daily choices move you toward that goal?

You can use these prompts in a study journal, in a talk, or in a family home evening lesson.
