The Plan of Salvation – Phase by Phase

This page groups the main principles of the Plan of Salvation by phase. Scan the whole plan or focus on one part of your journey at a time. Use the “Scripture Insight” buttons to open verses and explanations.

Premortal Life

Before we were born, we lived as spirit children of Heavenly Parents. We learned, progressed, and prepared to come to earth.

Earth Life

In mortality, we receive bodies, make covenants, and learn to walk by faith in Jesus Christ.

Spirit World

After death, our spirits go to the spirit world, where the gospel is taught and God’s mercy continues.

Resurrection & Judgment

Through the Resurrection of Jesus Christ, all will rise and be judged with perfect fairness and mercy.

Kingdoms of Glory

In the end, we inherit a degree of glory according to our desires, choices, and covenants.
