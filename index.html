About Me

Hi! My name is Francesco Foresta, and I’m currently studying Web Frontend Development I (WDD 231) through BYU-Idaho. I’m passionate about creating clean, user-friendly, and responsive websites that make information easy to access and enjoyable to explore.

I have a background in Learning & Development and translation, where I’ve managed large training projects and worked with multilingual content. Those experiences have taught me how important good design, accessibility, and clear communication are—skills I now apply to my web development journey.

Through this course, I’m excited to strengthen my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, build interactive projects, and prepare for a future career that blends technology, creativity, and learning.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy serving others, playing games, exploring new tools, and traveling to discover new cultures.
